Trigonometry Formulas

ðŸ“œ Sum of Angle Formulas:

\[ \sin \left(A \pm B \right) =\sin A \cos B \pm \cos A \sin B \]
\[ \cos\left(A \pm B \right) = \cos A \cos B \pm \sin A \sin B \]
\[ \tan\left(A \pm B \right)= \frac{ \tan A \pm \tan B} {1 \mp \tan A \tan B} \]

ðŸ“œ Product-to-Sum Formulas:

\[ \sin A \sin B = \frac{1}{2} [\cos(A-B) - \cos(A+B)] \]
\[ \cos A \cos B = \frac{1}{2} [\cos(A+B) + \cos(A-B)] \]
\[ \sin A \cos B = \frac{1}{2} [\sin(A+B) + \sin(A-B)] \]

ðŸ“œ Sum-to-Product Formulas:

\[ \sin A + \sin B = 2 \sin\left(\frac{A+B}{2}\right) \cos\left(\frac{A-B}{2}\right) \]
\[ \sin A - \sin B = 2 \cos\left(\frac{A+B}{2}\right) \sin\left(\frac{A-B}{2}\right) \]
\[ \cos A + \cos B = 2 \cos\left(\frac{A+B}{2}\right) \cos\left(\frac{A-B}{2}\right) \]
\[ \cos A - \cos B = -2 \sin\left(\frac{A+B}{2}\right) \sin\left(\frac{A-B}{2}\right) \]

ðŸ“œ Double Angle Formulas:

\[ \begin{align*} \sin 2A &= 2 \sin A \cos A \\ &= \frac{2 \tan A } { 1 + \tan^2 A } \end{align*} \]
\[ \begin{align*} \cos 2A &= \cos^2 A - \sin^2 A \\ &= 2\cos^2 A - 1 \\ &= 1 - 2\sin^2 A \\ &= \frac{1 - \tan^2 A}{1 + \tan^2 A} \end{align*} \]
\[ \tan 2A = \frac{2\tan A}{1 - \tan^2 A} \]

ðŸ“œ Triple Angle Formula:

\[ \sin 3A = 3 \sin A - 4 \sin^3 A \]
\[ \cos 3A = 4 \cos^3 A - 3 \cos A \]
\[ \tan 3A = \frac{ 3 \tan A - \tan^3 A} { 1 - 3 \tan^2 A } \]

ðŸ“œ Cube Formula:

\[ \sin^3 A = \frac{1} {4} (3 \sin A - \sin 3A ) \]
\[ \sin^3 A = \frac{1} {4} (3 \sin A - \sin 3A ) \]
\[ \tan^3 A = 3 \tan A + (3 \tan^2 A -1 )\tan 3A \]

ðŸ“œ Square Formula:

\[ \sin^2 A = \frac{1} {2} (1 - \cos 2A) \]
\[ \cos^2 A = \frac{1} {2} (1 - \cos 2A) \]
\[ \tan^2 A = 1 - \frac{ 2 \tan A } { \tan 2A} \]

ðŸ“œ Half Angle Formulas:

\[ \sin\left(\frac{A}{2}\right) = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos A}{2}} \]
\[ \cos\left(\frac{A}{2}\right) = \pm\sqrt{\frac{1+\cos A}{2}} \]
\[ \begin{align*} \tan\left(\frac{A}{2}\right) &= \pm\sqrt{\frac{1-\cos A}{1+\cos A}} \\ &= \frac{\sin A}{1+\cos A} \\ &= \frac{1-\cos A}{\sin A} \end{align*} \]

ðŸ“œ Basic Trigonometric Identities:

\[ \sin^2 A + \cos^2 A = 1 \]
\[ 1 + \tan^2 A = \sec^2 A \]
\[ 1 + \cot^2 A = \csc^2 A \]
